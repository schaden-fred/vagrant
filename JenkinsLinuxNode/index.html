This vagrantfile starts up a Centos 7 VM, configures and secures SSHD, installs maven, epel, nodejs, and phantomjs. The latest stable jenkins.war is downloaded to ~vagrant. The repo https://github.com/g0t4/jenkins2-course-spring-boot.git is cloned to ~vagrant/jenkins2/ The completed VM will be useful for anyone taking Wes Highbee's "Getting Started with Jenkins" course located here. https://app.pluralsight.com/library/courses/jenkins-2-getting-started/table-of-contents
